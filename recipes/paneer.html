Paneer

Description:

It's easy to make your very own paneer to use in your favorite Indian recipes. For homemade paneer, all you need is milk, lemon juice, salt, and a thermometer!

Ingredients:

  • 4 quarts whole milk
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt (Optional)
  • ½ cup lemon juice

Steps:

  1. Line a colander with a damp piece of muslin or cheesecloth and set in the sink or over a bowl.
  2. Pour milk into a large saucepan. Heat over medium-low heat to 200 degrees F (93 degrees C). Stir well and add salt. Pour in lemon juice gradually, 1 tablespoon at a time. Stir until curds separate.
  3. Ladle curds into the prepared colander. Fold muslin over curds and place a weight on top (such as a bowl filled with water). Press lightly and let drain for 2 to 3 hours.
